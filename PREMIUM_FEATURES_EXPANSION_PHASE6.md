## Risk Mitigation (Phase 6)

### Technical Risks
- **Video Infrastructure**: Use WebRTC + third-party SFU (Jitsi/LiveKit)
  - Mitigation: Tier video quality by plan (720p Basic, 1080p Premium)
  
- **Data Export at Scale**: Large exports could overload servers
  - Mitigation: Queue system, background processing, rate limits

### Business Risks
- **Feature Saturation**: 87 features might overwhelm users
  - Mitigation: Contextual discovery, role-based feature visibility
  - Strategy: Onboarding flows highlighting relevant features

- **Support Load**: Priority support promises increase support costs
  - Mitigation: Self-service knowledge base, AI chatbot tier
  - Strategy: Premium pricing includes support costs

### User Experience Risks
- **Complexity Overload**: Too many options confuse users
  - Mitigation: Smart defaults, progressive disclosure, guided tours
  - Strategy: Role-based feature presets
